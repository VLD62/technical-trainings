# Exercise 4 – Alert Design

## Goal

Design actionable alerts without creating unnecessary noise.

## Tasks

Review the included rules:

- InstanceDown
- HighCpuUsage
- HighMemoryUsage
- LowDiskSpace
- SampleAppHighErrorRate

For each rule decide:

1. Is the condition actionable?
2. Is the `for` duration appropriate?
3. What severity should be used?
4. Who owns the alert?
5. What troubleshooting step should appear in a runbook?
6. What could cause a false positive?

## Challenge

Design one symptom-based application alert and one infrastructure warning.
